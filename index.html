The EVFY Sustainability Roadmap

Our Sustainability Index is a program that provides green conscious members the platform to make informed carbon reduction decisions, with levels of services that consumers are comfortable with.

As part of the program, EVFY offers consumers subscription tiers and a roadmap to make the most informed decisions. This includes monthly reports, access to green products and services and offers a forum for members to share green lifestyle tips and a community to help others to become more ‘green.'

Our Partners

Piconi Farms

Piconi Farms was built on the belief that meals should not only be from our farm to your table, but organic and sustainable – and most importantly as special as possible for you and your family. We carry this belief into everything we do.Piconi Farms speciality is home grown freshness and flavors created with the ingenuity and passion inspired by years of family tradition.

Early Bird Wines

Early Bird Wine is America’s premier no additional sulfite wine. We produce our wines using a proprietary green technology, which results in a cleaner, fresher tasting wine with enhanced flavors and increased antioxidants for added health benefits and reduced risk of allergic reactions. Utilizing a 100% natural process, no added sulfites, and no chemical components, we are able to provide an affordable quality wine for you to enjoy, day and night, as part of your active, healthy lifestyle.
